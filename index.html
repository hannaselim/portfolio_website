Third Culture Kid.

Egyptian blood, Danish passport, Bahraini birthplace, Unknown enunciation

Filmmaker.

Throughout childhood I always knew I had a creative mind and an eye for scenery so I participated in every play at school. The role didn't matter, so long as I was on stage and had an audience I showed off my missing teeth in a smile. Growing up, I became bored of following directions, so I decided to go behind the scenes and I discovered filming and editing.

Student.

I am currently in my first semester of Multimedia Design and Communication at KEA - Copenhagen School of Design and Technology. In my spare time I borrow the tech lounge's camera and create videos and short films.
